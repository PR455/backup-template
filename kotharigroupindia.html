  1. 1980

    Founder chairman Mr. Kiran Kothari starts trading through a fertiliser shop

  2. 1985

    Diversifies into agriculture irrigation products trading and services

  3. 1997

    Manufacturing of PVC pipes & fittings begins with a single machine in unit 1

  4. 1999

    Starts unit 2 for manufacturing of hose pipes

  5. 2002

    Forays into Agri submersible cables

  6. 2003

    Starts unit 3 for HDPE pipes & fittings

  7. 2004

    Enters micro irrigation by starting unit 4 for irrigation systems

  8. 2005

    Exports first consignment to East Africa

  9. 2010

    Remodels unit 1 into India’s first 100% automised plant with technical know how from Europe expands market to Andhra Pradesh, Tamil Nadu, Gujarat, MadhyaPradesh, Chhatisgarh and Rajasthan

  10. 2011

    Achieves 100 crore turnover

  11. 2013

    Starts manufacturing of column pipes at the 100% automized unit 5

  12. 2014
